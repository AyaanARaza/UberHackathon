Driver EcoScore

Our Mission

Welcome to Driver EcoScore. Our mission is to offer users a way to track their driving habits and the results of these habits. The goal of EcoScore is to empower our users with eco-friendly solutions to enhance their EcoScore by providing alternate solutions to their current modes of transportation. We believe that our application can help the users in contributing to a greener environment. Additionally, some of the changes suggested by this application can result in reduced emissions and fuel consumption, which saves money and is good for the environment.

- Team PathFinders
